How to make it

Below is the tried-and-true approach to making lasagna. It may take a lot of time and effort, but it's worth it!

The ingredients

Technically, this recipe is quite customisable, and that's part of its appeal. You can change some ingredients, but most lasagna contains the following:

  • Meat: sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef in this case
  • Onion and Garlic: known to provide tons of flavour
  • Tomato products: a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: adds a subtle sweetness to enhance the sauce
  • Herbs and Spices: this lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles: literally in the name, can be storebought or homemade
  • Cheese: Mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese, as well as ricotta, are not uncommon choices
  • Egg: helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it

Cooking procedure

  1. Make some meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles, usually by boiling in salted water
  3. Make a ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna in the order shown in the next section
  5. Cover with foil and bake, removing the foil halfway through
  6. Let it rest a moment before serving

Layering the lasagna

  1. Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat until you're out of sauce and noodles, then top with more Paremsan cheese
